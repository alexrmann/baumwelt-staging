Projects

Our firm brings extensive, relevant experience in the planning and design of parks, open spaces, and community-focused recreation areas, with particular strength in off-leash dog park design, sustainable landscape architecture, and accessible, inclusive environments. We’ve partnered with a broad range of municipal and public agencies—delivering projects that are community-driven, code-compliant, and cost-conscious.

Featured Projects

  • DeMolay Sandspit Park

    Signature waterfront park with ADA waterfront access.

  • Logger Baseball Field Conversion

  • Logger Softball Field Renovation

  • Lower Wapato Creek Habitat Project

    Twenty-acre estuarine and freshwater habitat restoration.

    A combined soccer and football field renovation.

  • SERA Campus Playground

    Campus playground based on universal design principles.
