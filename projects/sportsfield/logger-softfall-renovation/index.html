Logger Softball Field Renovation

Comprehensive transformation of the University of Puget Sound’s softball facility.

Baumwelt led the design for the comprehensive transformation of the University of Puget Sound’s softball facility, converting the traditional dirt field to synthetic turf while undertaking a full renovation of the adjacent sports plaza. The project was developed in close collaboration with university leadership and the softball coaching staff to address longstanding issues and elevate the overall quality of the facility.

The previous field and plaza layout presented a range of challenges—including poor ADA accessibility, worn infrastructure, and significant sightline obstructions that negatively affected the spectator experience. Through thoughtful planning and detailed engagement with stakeholders, Derrick’s team delivered a solution that enhances both performance and presence.

The renovated facility will offer full ADA compliance, increased bleacher seating, and improved sightlines that ensure spectators can now view the full game experience. The new design meets current NCAA requirements and supports high-level play while also boosting player safety, aesthetics, and overall pride in the facility. The project reflects a significant step forward in the university’s support for its softball program and creates a modern, welcoming environment for athletes, staff, and fans alike.
