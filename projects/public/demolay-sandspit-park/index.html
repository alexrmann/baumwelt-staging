DeMolay Sandspit Park

Signature waterfront park with ADA waterfront access.

A signature waterfront park for PenMet renowned for its natural beauty, the Tacoma DeMolay Sandspit Park project also evolved in complexity since the commencement of design.

Initially, the project was greeted with the prospect of a fairly contentious public outreach effort. However, through a transparent process that outlined project milestones and activities, Baumwelt developed a master plan that was supported by the Stakeholder committee and the community at large.

Now the project design is fully underway and scheduled for a first phase of construction in late spring of 2026 which will deliver ADA access down to the waterfront for all park visitors.
