PenMet Parks HQ Campus & Minigolf Renovation

A creative design for a minigolf course with a Pacific Northwest nautical theme.

The dark horse candidate for PenMet Parks Project-of-the-Year! And that is saying something considering the completion of the beautiful Community Recreation Center next door.

Baumwelt immersed themselves in the design and success of this project, bringing not only over a decade of experience in synthetic turf replacement projects for the course greens, but also in the creativity and cohesive design of the minigolf PNW nautical theme. When initial minigolf suppliers seemed to lack some of the high caliber products that this installation warranted, Baumwelt sought out a solution - a firm that provided features for Universal Studios and Disney.

The scope on this project changed several times, and required a dynamic design firm that was flexible for Parks needs and willing to tackle various issues. Derelict water feature with no as-builts? No problem! Irrigation that has been hacked up beyond repair? Problems solved. Wrong-way designs from the minigolf supplier? Designs revised to better fit! As you consider who to select for Tubby’s Trail Dog Park, we ask that you take a moment to step outside of HQ and bask in the grandeur of the new HQ campus and minigolf. Baumwelt will endeavor to provide the same level of innovation and follow through to this dog park project.
