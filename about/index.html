About

Learn all about us and what we do.

Who We Are

Baumwelt is a landscape architecture and environmental design firm powered by inspired individuals who seek a better world for all. Our projects are guided by high ideals and grounded in collaboration with clients and end-users to realize positive change and measurable impacts in their communities. From our home office in Tacoma's Hilltop neighborhood, we seek partnerships with those who share our drive to build just, lasting, unforgettable outdoor spaces, places and experiences.

Why We Build

Inspired by our Swiss-German heritage, Baumwelt is a portmanteau representing the critical components of our practice:

  • Baum = Tree

    A symbol of earth, health and life, trees are a critical component of functional and beautiful landscapes. Trees bring ecological gains by supporting all oxygen-dependent life on earth, but they also serve as visual icons of the environment and partners to humanity through their visual, structural, and renewable characteristics.

  • Welt = World

    The world is our medium and our home. We all occupy one world, so we must seek ways to coexist on this planet now if we hope to witness a livable future. Throughout the advancement of humanity, the world has remained a finite resource that we must respect and consider in all we do.

  • Bau = Build

    We are builders and doers who take initiative and see our designs constructed. We partner with those most capable of realizing the build-out of our plans. We also build foundations and relationships with others in an effort to deliver inclusive and thoughtful work.

  • Umwelt = Environment

    Umwelt, whose literal translation is environment, is a philosophical concept of the world as it is experienced by a particular organism. We believe in opening our eyes and setting aside any preconceptions we hold in an effort to holistically understand our projects, who they affect, and how they can better all life on Earth.

What We Build

Baumwelt offers the following services:

  • Master Planning

    Working with the client to identify key project needs and concerns through a design process to develop the optimum plan for the site.

  • Public Engagement

    Often a part of any thorough master planning process, we utilize various strategies to gather meaningful public feedback and guide the project to be reflective of the needs of the stakeholders.

  • Concept Design

    Providing simple design guidance and preliminary planning. This effort serves to advance the project’s development and serve as an early guide for the client. Often it leads into more thorough design documentation.

  • Sportsfield Design & Technical Consultation

    Knowledge of the nuanced components of both natural and synthetic sports fields and playing surfaces, including underdrainage components and shock attenuation systems.

  • Design Visualization & Project Graphics

    2D & 3D orthographics and illustrations which visually convey ideas to public audiences and may be used in fundraising efforts.

  • Cost Estimating

    Utilization of our industry knowledge and real-world bid results of recent projects to aid in estimating projected costs for a new project.

  • Construction Documentation & Administration

    Production of bid-ready project plans, specifications, and cost estimates. Knowledge and ability to lead regular construction meetings with contractors and stakeholders to ensure project is constructed according the requirements of the contract documents.

How We Build

We believe our commitment to the following principles delivers successful projects:

  • Excellent Communication

    The most critical component of any project, we strive not only to clearly communicate to you but to also carefully listen (to you and to all members impacted by the work).

  • Sensitivity To Environmental Impact

    Ecological footprint is analyzed on every effort we do, and clients are made aware of opportunities to incorporate environmentally positive strategies in their project.

  • Emphasis On Design Justice

    We strive to achieve equity in the design of a project, to be enjoyed by all users. We don’t impart our agenda; we seek to understand who the community is and how they can be served fairly by the new project.

  • Diligent Adherence To Schedule and Budget

    These items are set at the beginning of the project, and we check back regularly to ensure no factors affect this.

  • Guaranteed Results

    As sole founding principal and active manager of the firm, I alone have the ability to contribute all firm resources necessary to ensure success. I am reachable, a good communicator, and dedicated to deliver the best project for our clients.
